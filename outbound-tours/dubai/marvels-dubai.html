Marvels Dubai

Dubai is the largest city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai, known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and lively nightlife. It is a global business and transport hub that has grown from a small trading village to a futuristic metropolis, featuring iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa and attractions like indoor ski slopes and desert safaris. The city is a cultural melting pot due to its large expatriate population, and its economy is primarily driven by tourism, aviation, and real estate. Attractions range from indoor skiing and skydiving to desert safaris and world-class shopping in souks and malls. The Dubai Fountain, located at the foot of the Burj Khalifa, is a major draw with its choreographed water shows.
